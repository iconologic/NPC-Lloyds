Dear Association Member:

You are eligible for a new benefit through State Bar Association which can protect your income and retirement funds in the event of an illness or accident that prevents you from working. This new plan is offered by Lloyd's of London, owned by you, and is available with no health questions or exams during this initial enrollment period for eligible State Bar Association members.

Kate Smith
Executive Director, State Bar Association

The illustrative graph below is designed to help you consider the following:

  1. What coverage you currently have
  2. Determine whether your current coverage is sufficient or if you should consider the new Lloyd's Lump Sum Disability Protection Plan

If you currently have disability insurance, you may have a base level of protection. Depending on your personal situation, that may not be sufficient to maintain all aspects of lifestyle, future goals and retirement funds.


By clicking "No Thanks" you are waiving the new Lloyd's Disability Income Protection Plan
