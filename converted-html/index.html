Announcing an Exclusive New Benefit for
Eligible NATLE Members

As a primary source of income in your household, protecting your current and future earnings in the event of an illness or accident is essential – as it is the one asset which provides for everything else. As a NATLE member, you can now obtain a personal income protection policy, without health questions or providing financial statements, during our open enrollment window, open now through June 12.

Please take a moment to review your personal cost/benefit options and record your election, whether you decide to enroll or opt-out. On this site, you can also review sample policies and FAQs for both the Monthly benefit policy and the Lump Sum benefit policies. Should you have any questions, simply click the email link or call 888-888-8888 to speak directly with a service consultant.
